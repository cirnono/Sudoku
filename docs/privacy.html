纯净数独隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 12 日

本隐私政策说明“纯净数独”（以下简称“本应用”）如何处理与保护用户信息。使用本应用即表示您已阅读本政策。

1. 本地数据

本应用会在您的设备上保存游戏进度、候选数、计时、难度、配色、语言、快速模式设置和当前局的手动存档。这些数据用于恢复游戏和保留您的偏好，不会由开发者上传至自有服务器。卸载应用或清除应用数据会删除这些本地信息。

2. 广告服务

本应用使用 Google AdMob 展示底部横幅广告。Google Mobile Ads SDK 可能根据适用法律和您的同意状态处理设备信息、IP 地址、广告标识符、诊断信息以及广告展示和互动数据，用于投放、衡量、防止欺诈及改进广告服务。

本应用使用 Google User Messaging Platform（UMP）在适用地区收集和管理广告隐私选择，并默认请求非个性化广告。Google 对数据的处理受其隐私政策和广告政策约束。

3. 账号与开发者服务器

本应用不要求创建账号，不收集姓名、电话号码、通讯录、精确位置或用户输入的数独内容，也不运营用于存储用户个人数据的开发者服务器。

4. 儿童隐私

本应用不以儿童为主要目标用户，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。如您认为儿童向本应用提供了个人信息，请通过下方邮箱联系我们。

5. 您的选择

您可以通过设备系统设置清除本应用的存储数据或卸载本应用。适用地区的用户可以通过 Google 提供的隐私消息管理广告同意选择。您也可以在设备设置中限制或重置广告标识符。

6. 安全

我们采取合理措施减少不必要的数据处理，但任何电子存储或网络传输方式都无法保证绝对安全。

7. 政策更新

本政策可能因功能、法律或第三方服务变化而更新。更新后的政策会在本页面公布，并修改生效日期。

8. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有疑问，请发送邮件至 gugee.chen@gmail.com。

Pure Sudoku Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pure Sudoku (the “App”) handles and protects user information. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read this policy.

1. Local Data

The App stores game progress, candidates, timer data, difficulty, color palette, language, quick-mode preferences, and the current game's manual checkpoint on your device. This data is used to restore games and retain your preferences. It is not uploaded to a server operated by the developer. Uninstalling the App or clearing its app data removes this local information.

2. Advertising Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display a bottom banner advertisement. Subject to applicable law and your consent choices, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device information, IP address, advertising identifiers, diagnostic information, and ad impression and interaction data to serve and measure ads, prevent fraud, and improve advertising services.

The App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect and manage advertising privacy choices where required and requests non-personalized ads by default. Google's processing is governed by its Privacy Policy and Advertising Policies.

3. Accounts and Developer Servers

The App does not require an account, does not collect names, phone numbers, contacts, precise location, or Sudoku entries, and does not operate a developer server that stores users' personal data.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the email below.

5. Your Choices

You can clear the App's stored data through your device settings or uninstall the App. Users in applicable regions can manage advertising consent through Google's privacy message. You may also limit or reset the advertising identifier in your device settings.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to minimize unnecessary data processing, but no method of electronic storage or network transmission is completely secure.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated when features, laws, or third-party services change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, email gugee.chen@gmail.com.
